# 💳 Credit Card Financial Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview

This project presents an interactive Credit Card Financial Dashboard developed using Power BI and SQL to provide actionable insights into customer behavior, transaction patterns, revenue generation, and overall credit card business performance.

The dashboard enables stakeholders to monitor key performance indicators (KPIs), analyze customer segments, identify spending trends, and make data-driven business decisions.

---

## 🎯 Project Objective

To develop a comprehensive credit card dashboard that provides real-time insights into key performance metrics and trends, enabling stakeholders to monitor and analyze credit card operations effectively.

## 📊 Dashboard Features

### Customer Analytics Dashboard

Provides customer-centric insights including:

- Total Revenue Analysis
- Total Interest Earned
- Customer Income Analysis
- Customer Satisfaction Score (CSS)
- Revenue by Gender
- Revenue by Age Group
- Revenue by Occupation
- Revenue by Education Level
- Revenue by Marital Status
- Revenue by Salary Group
- Revenue by State
- Revenue by Dependent Count

### Transaction Analytics Dashboard

Provides transaction-focused insights including:

- Total Transaction Revenue
- Total Interest Earned
- Transaction Amount Analysis
- Transaction Count Analysis
- Quarterly Revenue Trends
- Quarterly Transaction Trends
- Revenue by Card Category
- Revenue by Expenditure Type
- Revenue by Customer Profession
- Revenue by Education Level
- Revenue by Card Usage Method
  - Swipe
  - Chip
  - Online
- Customer Acquisition Cost Analysis

## 📈 Key Business Insights

### Customer Insights

- Middle-aged customers (40–60 years) contribute the highest revenue.
- Self-employed and business professionals generate significant revenue.
- Graduate and High School educated customers form major revenue segments.
- Married customers contribute slightly higher revenue than single customers.

### Transaction Insights

- Swipe transactions generate the highest revenue.
- Bills, entertainment, and fuel expenses are major spending categories.
- Blue card category contributes the largest share of revenue.
- Q3 shows peak transaction volume and revenue generation.
